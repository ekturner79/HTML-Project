ABOUT US

Italian Kitchen is a nose-to-tail authentic Italian restaurant. Our Executive Chef, Tony Romano grew up in Napoli. Here he lived with his parents and often spent time in the kitchen with ‘Mamma’ helping prepare food for the whole family. He arrived in Las Vegas in 1967, opening the first Italian Kitchen location shortly thereafter.

Our menu is inspired by dishes that have been enjoyed for generations throughout Italy. We’re passionate about bringing you a true taste of Italy, so you can experience the love, food, and magic of this amazing country first-hand in our restaurant.
